About the Author

David and Morgan Schroeder fell in love with surfing in Southern California, riding the magical point breaks of Malibu. When they had their son, Vance—nicknamed “Little Wave”—David naturally became “Big Wave.” Together they read many children's books, but none used groovy surf lingo or captured the vibe they shared — so they wrote their own.

David collaborated with artist Mau Rendon to create surf-inspired illustrations full of good vibes and a touch of magic.

He hopes this book reaches families who care about the ocean, the environment, and nurturing a connection between kids and nature. He wants young readers everywhere to feel stoked about the sea and the world around them.
